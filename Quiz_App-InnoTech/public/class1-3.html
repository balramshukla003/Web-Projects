Moral Science Questions

  1. What does honesty mean to you?
    Answer: Honesty means being truthful and sincere.
  2. Why is kindness important in society?
    Answer: Kindness fosters empathy, cooperation, and a sense of community.
  3. What is the golden rule in moral science?
    Answer: Treat others the way you want to be treated.
  4. Why should we respect our elders?
    Answer: Elders have wisdom and experience, and respecting them helps maintain harmony.
  5. What is the difference between right and wrong?
    Answer: Right actions align with moral values, while wrong actions violate them.
  6. Why is it important to help others in need?
    Answer: Helping others builds a compassionate and caring society.
  7. What is the significance of forgiveness?
    Answer: Forgiveness helps in healing and maintaining peace in relationships.
  8. How can you show responsibility in daily life?
    Answer: By fulfilling your duties and being accountable for your actions.
  9. Why is it important to be punctual?
    Answer: Punctuality shows respect for others' time and helps in maintaining discipline.
  10. What is the value of gratitude?
    Answer: Gratitude helps us appreciate the good in life and fosters positive relationships.

  9. What do we call the variety of living organisms in a particular habitat?
